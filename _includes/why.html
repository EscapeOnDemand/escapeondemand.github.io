Why Escape On Demand?

We can have over 150 people go through our escape rooms in a single session and we offer the added benefit of coming to you. We offer 4 unique room themes so attendees can return and receive a different experience.

Escape rooms offer cooperation and team building opportunities. Teams must work together in order to make it out in time. Solving these unique puzzles also comes with a great sense of satisfaction that your group will be talking about long after the event.
