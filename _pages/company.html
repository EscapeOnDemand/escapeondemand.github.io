History

Escape On Demand was founded with a lofty objective: to offer large groups the opportunity to participate in escape rooms which were previously reserved for groups no larger than 40 members.

There was also a lack of alternative team-building options.

We created Escape On Demand to provide an alternative that focused on the essentials of team-building.

Our vision and business idea At IKEA our vision is to create a better everyday life for the many people. Our business idea supports this vision by offering a wide range of well-designed, functional home furnishing products at prices so low that as many people as possible will be able to afford them.
